About Me

Deepak N — Junior Legal Counsel Transitioning into Privacy

I am Deepak N, a B.A.LL.B. (Hons.) graduate from SRM School of Law, Chennai, with legal experience spanning contract management, data privacy, and GDPR compliance. I am currently actively exploring roles in Data Privacy and Privacy Operations.

My previous experience as Junior Legal Counsel at Teceze Consultancy Services included independently authoring a GDPR-compliant Data Privacy Policy, drafting and reviewing Data Processing Agreements, and advising on controller-processor obligations, Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs), and cross-border data transfer mechanisms under GDPR and UK GDPR.

This portfolio bridges my legal and privacy background into hands-on privacy operations work — the data mapping, ROPA management, vendor risk review, and gap analysis that privacy analysts perform daily inside in-house privacy teams.

Why I'm transitioning: Drafting DPAs and reviewing SCCs at Teceze is what first pulled me toward privacy operations. I realised I wanted to move from advising on agreements to actually operationalising them — building the registers, assessments, and workflows that sit behind the clauses. This portfolio is how I'm bridging that gap, one work product at a time.

"I have drafted real DPAs, advised on SCCs, and built a company's data privacy policy from scratch. This portfolio shows what I can do operationally."
